About

Hey, I'm Jamal.

I've been trading for 5+ years, starting in forex before finding my edge in futures — specifically MNQ, where precision and speed meet.

I'm self-taught. YouTube, screen time, and a lot of expensive lessons. Nobody handed me a system. I built one.

Trading pulled me in because of leverage — not just financial leverage, but the leverage of skill, discipline, and decision-making. It's one of the few arenas where results are purely yours. No excuses, no hiding. The market doesn't care about your feelings.

But the deeper obsession is this: most people approach trading completely wrong. They chase wins, blow accounts, and never build anything real. I've learned that trading done right is about structure, patience, and longevity — shifting from gambling to building a skillset that compounds over time.

The hardest lesson? Consistency isn't built from big wins. It's built from controlling losses. One moment of impatience can erase weeks of solid execution. Trading forced me to look at myself honestly — my emotions, my habits, my decision-making under pressure. And once you understand that, you realize trading is less about the market and more about mastering yourself.
