How it works

Four steps from idea to delivered print. No surprise fees.

1

Send the brief

Pick from the catalog or upload your STL/OBJ. Tell us material, color, and size.

2

Get a quote

You’ll get a fixed price and lead time within 24 hours, with a print preview.

3

We print & finish

Calibrated machines, supports removed, sanded if needed, light bead-blasting on resin.

4

Ship or pick up

Padded packaging, tracked shipping, or local pickup. Most orders ship within 48 hours.
