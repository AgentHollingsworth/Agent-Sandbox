Entry Rules
Two setups — trend continuation and counter trend
Trend Continuation
With the cloud
1
Higher TF cloud confirms direction. Trade with the cloud — longs in white cloud, shorts in black cloud.
2
PMax crosses same direction on lower TF. Wait for the PMax signal on the entry chart to align with cloud bias.
3
Stop = PMax line Blue ATR — place stop at the blue PMax line at time of entry.
4
Trail stop with PMax until the trend ends. Do not exit early — let the PMax line take you out.
Counter Trend
Against the cloud — stricter criteria
1
Price must be outside the cloud. Never take a counter trend trade while price is inside the cloud.
2
TDI divergence must be present. Confirm momentum divergence on the TDI before considering entry.
3
Price must close above/below the yellow PMax line Yellow PMax — the close confirms the reversal signal.
4
Stop = below/above the entry candle. Tight stop on the trigger candle.
5
Target 1 = 50 SMA  /  Target 2 = 200 SMA. Scale out at the 50, let runners go to the 200.
⚠️ Hard Rule: Never take a counter trend trade if price is inside the cloud. All three conditions must be met before entry.
